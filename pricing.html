FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime? +

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract or lock-in period? +

No contracts, no lock-in. All our plans are pay-as-you-go. You can cancel anytime and your service continues until the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and bank transfers. For enterprise plans, we also support invoice-based payments with custom billing cycles.

Do you offer refunds? +

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund — no questions asked.

What's included in the free trial? +

The free trial gives you full access to core features for 30 days — including dashboard, student/inventory management, reports, and email support. No credit card required to start.

Can I get a custom plan? +

Absolutely! If none of our standard plans fit your needs, contact our sales team and we'll create a custom plan tailored to your institution or business requirements.
