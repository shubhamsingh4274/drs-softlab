FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DRS Softlabs? +

DRS Softlabs is a technology company based in Noida, India. We build AI-powered SaaS platforms for Indian businesses — currently NeoShala (school management) and Aushadhi (pharmacy management).

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! Both NeoShala and Aushadhi offer free trials — 30 days for NeoShala and 15 days for Aushadhi. No credit card required.

How do I get support? +

Direct WhatsApp support from the founding team! No tickets, no bots. Call us at +91 94533 73823 or email drssoftlabs@gmail.com. We respond within hours.

Is my data safe? +

Absolutely. We use encryption, 2FA, and never share your data with third parties. You can export all your data as CSV anytime. Your data belongs to you.

Do you support multiple languages? +

Yes! 10+ Indian languages including Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Bengali, Gujarati, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, and Punjabi.

Can I use it on my phone? +

Yes! Both NeoShala and Aushadhi are fully responsive — they work beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile phones. No app download needed.

How long does setup take? +

Less than 10 minutes! Sign up, add your institute/store details, and start using immediately. We also offer free setup assistance via WhatsApp if you need help importing existing data.

Can I manage multiple branches/stores? +

Absolutely! Both products support multi-branch management. One super admin can oversee multiple schools or pharmacy stores from a single dashboard with separate data for each.

What about GST compliance? +

Aushadhi auto-calculates GST, CGST, SGST on every bill. Generate GSTR-1 reports with one click. All invoices are GST-compliant with HSN codes. Perfect for pharmacy audits.

Can I generate PDF reports and certificates? +

Yes! NeoShala generates certificates (TC, bonafide, character), report cards, fee receipts, attendance reports, salary slips, and teacher attendance PDFs — all branded with your school logo. Aushadhi generates GST bills, purchase orders, and sales reports.

What makes you different from Entab, Fedena, or Marg ERP? +

Three things: (1) We're 5-10x cheaper with zero setup fees, (2) Direct founder support via WhatsApp — no ticketing systems, (3) Modern UI with WhatsApp integration, 10+ languages, and AI analytics that legacy software simply doesn't offer.

Yes! NeoShala has a student/parent portal where they can check attendance, exam results, fee status, timetable, and download report cards. Teachers can mark attendance and upload marks from their own login.

Does NeoShala support transport route management? +

Yes! Full transport module — define routes, stops, assign students to buses, track transport fees separately, and generate route-wise reports. Parents get WhatsApp updates about bus timings.

Can I manage teacher salary and HR with NeoShala? +

Absolutely! NeoShala includes HR/Payroll — teacher profiles, clock-in attendance, salary calculation with deductions, auto-generated salary slips (PDF), and payroll reports. All in one place.

How does Aushadhi handle expiry tracking? +

Aushadhi uses FEFO (First Expiry First Out) for automatic batch selection during billing. Get alerts 90/60/30 days before expiry. Dashboard shows expiring medicines with return options. This alone saves pharmacies lakhs every year.

Can I manage credit (Udhar) customers in Aushadhi? +

Yes! Full credit ledger — track who owes what, partial payment support, auto WhatsApp payment reminders, customer-wise outstanding reports, and doctor-wise credit tracking. Our users reported 60% reduction in outstanding within the first month.

Does Aushadhi support barcode scanning? +

Yes! Scan barcodes with any USB barcode scanner or your phone camera. Medicine auto-populates in the billing screen. You can also print custom barcode labels for medicines that don't have one.

Can I run an online pharmacy shop with Aushadhi? +

Yes! Aushadhi includes a built-in online shop module. Customers can browse medicines, place orders via WhatsApp or your custom URL, and you manage orders from the same dashboard. No separate website needed.
