Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Shubham and his team in Noida — on a mission to digitize every Indian business.

The Beginning

Started with a Simple Idea

In 2025, Shubham Singh noticed something — Indian businesses were either using outdated software or expensive foreign tools that didn't understand Indian workflows.

GST compliance, Udhar/Credit systems, multi-language needs, WhatsApp integration — none of the existing tools got it right. So he built DRS Softlabs from his home in Noida.

Today, 500+ businesses across India trust DRS Softlabs to run their daily operations.
