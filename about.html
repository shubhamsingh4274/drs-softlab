The Beginning

Started with a Simple Idea

In 2026, Shubham Singh noticed something — Indian businesses were either using outdated software or expensive foreign tools that didn't understand Indian workflows.

GST compliance, Udhar/Credit systems, multi-language needs, WhatsApp integration — none of the existing tools got it right. So he built DRS SoftLab from his home in Noida.

Today, 500+ businesses across India trust DRS SoftLab to run their daily operations.
